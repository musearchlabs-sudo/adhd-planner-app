Short version: We use almost no cookies. No tracking, no ads, no creepy stuff. Just the bare minimum to keep the site running.
01
What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use them. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies we use and why.

02
Cookies We Use
Cookie Name Type Purpose Duration
__cf_bm Essential Cloudflare bot management — protects the site from automated abuse 30 minutes
cf_clearance Essential Cloudflare challenge verification — proves you are human 1 year
adhd_planner_* Functional localStorage (not a cookie) — stores your daily planner data locally in your browser only Per session/day

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising cookies.

03
localStorage vs Cookies

Your planner data (tasks, habits, mood, etc.) is stored in browser localStorage, which is technically different from cookies. localStorage:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is never sent to our servers
  • Is not accessible by us or any third party
  • Can be deleted by clearing your browser data
04
Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Here's how on the most common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Disabling essential Cloudflare cookies may affect site security but will not break the planner functionality.
