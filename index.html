FAQ

QUESTIONS
ANSWERED

Is this really free? +
Yes, completely free. No freemium, no trial period, no hidden cost. Open it, use it, come back tomorrow. That's it.
Do I need to create an account? +
No account needed at all. Just open the link and start using it. Your progress saves automatically in your browser's local storage — no server, no data collection.
Does it work on my phone? +
Yes — it's fully responsive and tested on mobile, tablet, and desktop. The layout adapts to every screen size. You can even add it to your home screen like an app.
Will it remember my data the next day? +
It saves your data for the current day automatically every 30 seconds. Each new day starts fresh — which is intentional, because ADHD brains do better with a clean slate each morning.
Do I need to install anything? +
Nothing to install. It runs in your browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge — all work perfectly. Optionally, you can save it to your home screen on iPhone or Android for one-tap access.
I don't have an ADHD diagnosis — can I still use it? +
Absolutely. Many people with undiagnosed ADHD, anxiety, or who simply struggle with focus find it helpful. The techniques (brain dump, Pomodoro, habit streaks) work for most people — ADHD diagnosis or not.
