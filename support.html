Why This Exists

THE STORY
BEHIND THIS

This planner was built out of personal frustration. Every planner, app, and system out there was designed for neurotypical brains — neat little boxes, rigid schedules, no room for the chaos of an ADHD morning.

So we built something different. A tool that starts with a brain dump instead of a to-do list. That uses Pomodoro timers because ADHD brains need hard stops. That includes a medication log because forgetting is part of the condition, not a character flaw.

And we made it free — because the people who need it most often can't afford yet another subscription.

Running this costs real money: hosting, domain, maintenance, future development. Your support — however small — directly funds the time and infrastructure that keeps this free for thousands of people.

QUESTIONS

Is my donation tax deductible? +
No — this is a personal project, not a registered charity. Donations are voluntary contributions to support an independent creator, not tax-deductible donations.
What if I can't afford to donate? +
Please use the app for free — that's exactly what it's here for. No guilt, no pressure. If you want to support for free, sharing the link with someone who might benefit is just as valuable.
Will the app remain free forever? +
Yes, the core planner will always be free. If we ever add premium features, they'll be genuinely optional — the full daily planner will never go behind a paywall.
Can I get a refund on a donation? +
Donations are non-refundable as they're voluntary gifts. If you made an error (wrong amount, accidental payment), contact us and we'll do our best to help.
What's the best way to support for free? +
Share the link in ADHD communities, leave a review on our Etsy shop, tell your therapist or doctor about it, or post about it on social media. Word of mouth keeps this project alive.
