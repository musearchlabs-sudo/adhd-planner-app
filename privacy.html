01
Overview

ADHD Daily Planner ("we", "us", "our") operates the website and web application available at this domain. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our free planner tool and visit our website.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This tool was built for people managing ADHD — we understand that health-related data is sensitive, and we've designed the app so that your data never leaves your device.

02
What Data We Collect

Data you enter in the planner (brain dump, tasks, habits, medication logs, etc.): This is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage. It is never transmitted to our servers, never seen by us, and never shared with anyone.

Data collected automatically when you visit:

  • IP address (anonymised, via Cloudflare infrastructure)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent (aggregate, anonymised analytics only)
  • Referring URL

Data we do NOT collect:

  • Your name or email address (no account required)
  • Your ADHD diagnosis or health information
  • Your medication names or dosages
  • Your tasks, habits, or planner entries
  • Payment information (we don't process payments on this site)
Key fact: The planner works entirely client-side. Your data is stored using localStorage in your own browser — the same technology websites use to remember your preferences.
03
How We Use Your Data

The limited anonymised technical data we collect is used solely to:

  • Understand how many people use the app (aggregate counts only)
  • Identify and fix technical errors
  • Improve the performance and usability of the app
  • Protect the website against abuse

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose other than the above.

04
Data Storage

All planner data (your tasks, habits, mood, medication logs, reflections) is stored in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • It stays on your device only
  • Clearing your browser cache or history will delete it
  • It is not synced across devices
  • We have no access to it whatsoever

Our website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages, which may retain server access logs for up to 30 days for security purposes. These logs contain anonymised IP addresses and are not linked to any personal identity.

05
Cookies

We use minimal cookies. Specifically:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function correctly (e.g. Cloudflare security cookies).
  • No tracking cookies: We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any third-party tracking cookies.
  • No advertising cookies: We run no ads and use no ad network cookies.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. This will not affect the core functionality of the planner.

06
Third Parties

We use the following third-party services to operate this website:

  • Cloudflare Pages — website hosting and CDN. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies to infrastructure-level data.
  • Google Fonts — we load fonts (Bebas Neue, Plus Jakarta Sans) from Google's CDN. This may result in your IP being sent to Google's servers. You can disable this by using a browser extension like uBlock Origin.

We do not use Facebook, TikTok, or any social media tracking pixels on this website.

07
Your Rights

Under GDPR and UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request what personal data we hold about you
  • Erasure — request deletion of any data we hold
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to how we process your data
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data

Because we collect almost no personal data, most of these rights have limited practical application. To exercise any right or ask a question, contact us at the address below.

Note: We cannot access or delete data stored in your own browser's localStorage. To delete your planner data, clear your browser's local storage or use your browser's "Clear Site Data" option.
08
Children's Privacy

This service is intended for users aged 13 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used this service and provided any personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to address this.
